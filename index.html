About Me

I swore up and down that I wanted to be a doctor until the 11th grade, where in an IT class we were introduced to programming. I realized I really liked it, and it seemed to like me too. I also came to realize that though I was good at the sciences, blood made me queezy.

I decided to pursue a Bachelors degree in Computer Science and put in the work until that goal was accomplished in 2021. I enjoy making things. Especially things that have a purpose and help other people. This passion lead me to choose to move half way across the world to be apart the very "hands on" Graduate Full Stack program at NSCC. So far its been a blast and I have been loving my decision as I feel this course allows me to learn more and do more of what I enjoy; making meaningful things.

I also aspire to join a local team to both learn and assist in the accomplishment of our given objectives. As a very driven person who considers herself a lifelong learner, I look forward to learning React and MongoDB specifically focusing on that part of the stack.

Keep Learning. Keep growing.
